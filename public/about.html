About StudAI Research Project

Welcome to this experimental platform exploring human-AI interaction.

Project Overview

This is an open research experiment investigating the effects of an unrestricted acces to users to teaching an ai model.

Research Objective

The project aims to answer:

  • "What would humanity teach an AI if there were no constraints?"
  • Ethical considerations in open-ended AI training

How can I participate?

  • You can talk freely with studAi without having to teach him anything. He just wants to have a chat sometimes :)
  • You can teach him anything you want -from the most controversial opinion or false fact to the most valuable piece of information ever told-
  • You can ask him different questions to test his knowledge

Limitations

  • Each session has a maximum of 10 messages. This is done for limiting the pressure over the database and the APIs
  • Given the fact that the ai base model is an open-source model (llama-3.3-70b-versatile) it may sometimes behave in a unwanted way
  • The web uses some API services for running - Expect possible downtimes
